Why Choose Us?

Whether you are a large law firm or an unrepresented individual, our team of highly experienced and properly licensed Process Servers will ensure that your documents will be served quickly, reliably and correctly.

  • We utilize an advanced Service Management system which allows Servers in the field to make real-time updates of attempts and completed services.
  • This system is fully compliant with the New York City Department of Consumer Affairs Regulations including GPS tagging of every attempt and every service.
  • Our system allows for you to have access to the real-time up to the minute status of all of your ongoing services as well as a searchable history of all of your prior services.
