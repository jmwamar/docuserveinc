Skip Tracing

If an individual who needs to be served with legal documents has disappeared, planning a court action can be a hard task to accomplish. It may be extremely difficult to learn where the individual is living – when a person knows they must appear in court, they may relocate to a different state (or even a different country), change their name or take other measures in an attempt to hide. Among the advantages of working with are process service agency is that we can offer professional skip tracing services. We can utilize various methods to find individuals who have disappeared in order to properly serve them with legal documents.

Attempting to locate an individual to be served will likely involve a great deal of money, time, and effort for a task you may not be able to accomplish on your own. You need to access records and documents that are difficult to find, as well as the ability to utilize resources that only legal professionals have at their fingertips.

Why Choose a Professional?

  • Skip tracing requires gathering every bit of information regarding an individual in order to learn where they are.
  • There is usually a trail of facts that can be followed to find someone so that papers may be served.
  • A seasoned professional in the process service industry can access high-tech, innovative surveillance techniques, insider information and methods to accurately locate the individual to be served.

We specialize in Government and Corporate Service of process in Albany, NY including:

  • The NY Secretary of State
  • Vehicle and Traffic Law 253/254 (Service of a Summons on a non-resident or a resident who has departed the state of New York)
  • All Government Agencies
  • NY State Department of Taxation & Finance
  • The NY State Attorney General’s Office
  • All Registered Agents Including Corporation Service Company
